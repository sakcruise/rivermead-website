About Us

More than a club.
A community.

Rivermead Badminton Club is one of Reading's most competitive clubs, fielding three teams in the Berkshire County Badminton League: Men's A, Men's B, and a Mixed team. We compete against 15+ clubs across the county divisions every season.

Whether you're looking to sharpen your game, earn a spot in one of our league teams, or just enjoy a great Tuesday night out on the courts. There's a place for you at Rivermead.

✓ Berkshire County League
✓ 3 competitive teams
✓ All abilities welcome
✓ BA England affiliated
🏆

Men's A Team

Our top squad competing in the higher Berkshire county divisions against the best clubs in the region.

🥈

Men's B Team

A strong second squad giving more players the chance to represent the club in county league fixtures.

🎽

Mixed Team

Men and women competing together in Berkshire's mixed doubles division, one of our most exciting teams.
